# AI Usage Declaration

This project was primarily built by the human developer. Around 90% of the planning, direction, design choices, feature decisions, testing, review, and final implementation control were handled by the developer.

AI assistance was used in a limited supporting role, estimated at around 10% of the overall work.

## AI Role

- AI helped with minor code suggestions, debugging support, formatting improvements, and documentation wording.
- AI was used as an assistant, not as the main creator or decision-maker.
- All important product decisions, credentials, feature requirements, visual direction, and final approvals came from the human developer.

## Human Role

- The human developer created the project idea and controlled the full development direction.
- The human developer provided the requirements, API credentials, design direction, product links, and feedback.
- The human developer reviewed, tested, edited, and approved the final output.

## Areas Where AI Assisted

- Small parts of React component structure and styling suggestions.
- FastAPI route scaffolding support.
- Debugging help for API, OAuth, and frontend layout issues.
- Documentation wording and cleanup.

## Notes

- Secrets and API keys are expected to remain in local `.env` files only.
- Any AI-assisted output was reviewed by the human developer before use.
- The project should be reviewed again before production deployment, especially authentication, product links, and astrological recommendation logic.
